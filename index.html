To All Our Classmates,

It is said that time passes more quickly the older we get. That likely feels true for most, if not all, of us from Madison West Class of 1971.        

All the more reason that we hope the turnout for our 55th high school reunion on Saturday, October 10, 2026, will be huge.

For our 50th reunion, there were 150 attendees at the Saturday evening event, and that was fantastic, especially in the middle of the COVID epidemic. For our 55th reunion, we will have a tour of West High School Saturday morning, a golf outing Saturday midday, and an evening event. It will be a great time to reconnect, get reacquainted, and even meet some classmates you didn’t know at all back in the day.

There were 577 members in our class. To date, 79 have passed on. Time marches on. 

Please join us to celebrate your 55th high school reunion. You are part of what made our class special.

Your Reunion Committee
