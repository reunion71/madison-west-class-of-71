To All Our Classmates,

It is hard to believe that half a century has passed since our graduation ceremony. So much has changed over the years on the world stage and certainly for every member of the Madison West High School Class of 1971.

Only once in our lifetimes will we arrive as we have now to celebrate our 50th high school class reunion. We hope you can plan to be in Madison on September 17 and 18 to reconnect, remember and rejoice.

Whoever you were in high school, whatever you are now, we hope you will join us. Our high school experience was not the same for everyone. You may have been an athlete, a hippie, a bookworm, activist, or simply felt left out. You might have been popular, or troubled. Perhaps a cheerleader, or someone in the stands. Maybe you never went to a game. You may have been a nerd, a scholar, a loner, a stoner, or just wanted to get through. It might have been one big blur or possibly one of the best times of your life.

No matter who you were, you are a part of what made our class special.

Let's come together and celebrate the years as we are the Class of 1971! Be there.

Your Reunion Committee

P.S. Please share our website with any classmate you may know. We look forward to seeing you in September
